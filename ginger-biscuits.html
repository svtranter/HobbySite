Ginger Biscuits

A recipe by Jann Evans

Ingredients

  • 250g soft margarine
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup syrup
  • 4 cups flour
  • 2 tsp ginger
  • 2 tsp bicarb
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp allspice

Method

  1. Sieve all dry ingredients together.
  2. Beat marge, sugar and eggs together add syrup. Combine with dry ingredients.
  3. Form into walnut size balls.
  4. Place on baking tray, squash with fork.
  5. Bake at 350â„‰ till golden brown.
  6. Remove from baking tray while hot.
  7. When cool store in sealed container.

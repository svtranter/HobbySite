Whitebread

A recipe by Keryn Parker

Ingredients

  • 10g Gold Star Instant Dry Yeast
  • 1Kg Flour
  • 15ml Salt
  • 10ml Sugar
  • 20g Margarine
  • 650ml Lukewarm Water

Method

  1. Place all the dry ingredients in the mixing bowl and mix together.
  2. Pour in water and mix.
  3. Knead until a soft dough is formed.
  4. Place the dough on slightly floured surface, cover with a sheet of plastic and allow to rest for 20 mins.
  5. Knock down and divide dough into two. Shape and place on bread tins leave until double in size.
  6. Bake for 35 mins.

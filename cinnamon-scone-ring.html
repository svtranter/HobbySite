Cinnamon Scone Ring

A recipe by Jann Evans

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 Tsp sugar
  • Pinch salt
  • ¾ cup icing sugar
  • 60g butter/marg
  • 3t baking powder
  • About ½ cup milk
  • Raisins

Method

  1. Sift dry ingredients.
  2. Rub in butter/marg.
  3. Add milk.
  4. Mix to form a scone consistency.
  5. Roll out on lightly floured surface.
  6. Spread thinly with soft butter/marg.
  7. Sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar mixture (add raisins if desired).
  8. Roll as for swiss roll.
  9. Cut into slices about 2cm thick.
  10. Arrange in cake tin.
  11. Bake at 180℃ till golden brown (about 20 mins).

Topping

  1. Mix ¾ cup icing sugar with a little water.
  2. Pour over scone ring while still very hot.

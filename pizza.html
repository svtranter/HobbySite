Pizza

A recipe by Keryn Parker
Makes 4 pizzas
Oven temperature 230â„ƒ

Ingredients

  • 10g Gold Star Instant Dry Yeast
  • 500g Cake Flour
  • 10ml Salt
  • 20ml Sugar
  • 340ml Lukewarm Water

Method

  1. Combine the flour, salt & sugar together. Add the yeast.
  2. Gradually add the liquid and mix well to form a soft, pliable dough. Knead well for 10 minutes until the dough is smooth and elastic.
  3. Place the dough on a lightly floured surface, cover with a sheet of plastic and allow to rest for 20mins.
  4. Divide the dough into 4 equal pieces and roll each piece into a round (shape) approx. 23cm in diameter. Place onto a greased baking tray or greased oven proof plates.
  5. Cover with selected toppings, eg. sliced tinned tomato, dried herbs, olives, anchovies, capers, salami, etc.
  6. Cover with a sheet of plastic and allow to prove for 30 mins in a warm place.
  7. Bake in a preheated oven for 15 to 20 mins.
